Call for Papers

We invite submissions of papers for the Workshop on Generative AI and FAIR Principles in Science Communication (AI4SC-2024). Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Generative AI in scientific research
  • FAIR principles in AI development
  • Interdisciplinary approaches to AI in science communication
  • Case studies and applications
  • Ethical and Equity Challenges in LLM for Scientific Communication
  • Leveraging LLM to Democratize Access to Science
  • Integrating Knowledge Graphs and Ontologies with LLM
  • Formal model of LLM for Information Assurance for Science Communications
  • LLM for Information Retrieval and Information Annotation
  • LLM Application for Database and Query Processing for Scientific Result Representation

Please submit your papers by the deadline listed in the Important Dates section.
